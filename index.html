_ of 960

Chess 960, or Fischer Random Chess, is a chess variant where the back row is randomized at the start. There are two other constraints to the initial game state: * The King is placed on a square between the two rooks * The Bishops are placed on opposite colors This results in (eponymously) 960 board possibilities for the starting state. Aside from the initial conditions, there are modified rules for castling for which I'll refer you to the Wikipedia Page.
